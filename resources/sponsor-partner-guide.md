# Sponsor and Community Partner Guide

**Planned Summer 2026 Northwest Indiana hack days**

Local partners help make **beginner-friendly, phone-first** hackathons accessible to students who may not own laptops or paid software.

**Merrillville pilot — venue confirmation pending**

---

## Why this event matters

- Northwest Indiana students deserve **low-barrier** paths into tech  
- Many families cannot afford **expensive devices** for learning events  
- Community-built projects can address **real local needs** (schools, churches, small businesses, neighborhoods)  
- A positive first hackathon experience can change how young people see their future  

---

## Ways to partner

| Support type | Impact |
|--------------|--------|
| **Snacks and drinks** | Keeps energy up during a long build day |
| **Prizes** | Recognizes beginners, phone-first projects, community impact |
| **Printing** | Signs, schedules, worksheets, name tags |
| **Transportation support** | Helps students attend Merrillville/Gary-area events |
| **Device lending** | Chromebooks or tablets for participants without laptops |
| **Hotspot support** | Reliable internet for phone-first builders |
| **Workshop mentors** | Short skill sessions (pitching, Replit, AI basics, design) |
| **Venue help** | Space, tables, chairs, Wi-Fi coordination |

*Specific benefits depend on final sponsorship level and event budget. Nothing is guaranteed until agreements are signed.*

---
